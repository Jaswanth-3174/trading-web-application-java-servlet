Dashboard

Welcome to your trading dashboard. This platform helps you manage your investments, track market movements, and control your trading activities from one place.

The Market section allows you to explore live stock data, view the stocks order book, and select different market options to analyze prices and market depth before trading.

Using the Orders section, you can place buy and sell orders and track their status. It provides a complete view of pending, executed, and cancelled orders for better trade monitoring.

The Accounts section helps you manage your funds and holdings. You can view your balance, add money, check your portfolio, or delete an account by selecting an appropriate option.

The Reports section gives you access to transaction details and complete trading history. You can view individual transactions or all transactions together for performance analysis and record keeping.

Use the navigation menu to select a section and manage your trading activities efficiently with real-time updates and organized information.
